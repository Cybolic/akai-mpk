AKAI – MPK mini

CC + PROG CHANGE OFF

Play pads to trigger notes.

CC ON
PAD BANK 1
P 5/1 Previous/next track = cursor track.
P 6/2 Previous/next device = cursor device.
P 7/3 Transpose pads up/down.
PAD BANK 2
P 1-4 Toggle views.
Hold P 8 pressed and move a knob to full right to select a macro for mapping. do again to exit mapping.
P 7/3 Transpose pads up/down.
PROG CHANGE ON
PAD BANK 1
P 1 Toggle solo on selected track.
P 5 Toggle arm on selected track.
P 6/2 Select previous/next preset.
P 7/3 Select previous/next preset category.
P 8/4 Select previous/next preset creator.
PAD BANK 2
K 1-8 Macros of the cursor device.
